Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

LandMark.am does not collect, store or request any **personal data** from visitors.

We publish landscape and location photography publicly available on our website.

2. Use of Content

We publicly display our photographs on LandMark.am.

  • High-resolution images are available for commercial use only through a paid **Patreon membership**.
  • Low-resolution images are free for **non-commercial use** with proper credit.

3. Analytics and Cookies

LandMark.am uses **Cloudflare Analytics** to monitor website security and performance.

Cloudflare may process basic, non-identifiable technical information such as:

  • IP region (approximate)
  • device/browser type
  • page interactions

This data is **anonymous** and cannot identify individual users.

The website may use **essential cookies** provided automatically by the hosting and Cloudflare services.

4. Payments

All payments and subscriptions are handled exclusively through **Patreon**.

LandMark.am does not access, store, or process any payment information (such as credit cards, billing details, or personal identifiers).

All Patreon subscribers are subject to **Patreon’s own Privacy Policy**.

5. Data Storage

LandMark.am does not store any personal data on its servers.

6. Removal Requests

If a person appears in a photograph on LandMark.am, they may request the removal of the image at any time.

You can contact us and we will remove the image promptly.

7. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer user data to any third parties.
